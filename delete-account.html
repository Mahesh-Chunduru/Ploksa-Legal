Delete Your Ploksa Account

Ploksa allows you to delete your account and all associated app data at any time.

Delete from the app

You can delete your account directly from inside the Ploksa app by following this path:

Open Ploksa → Profile → Delete Account
!

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account removes your profile and all data linked to it, including:

  • Account profile information
  • Saved spaces
  • Rooms
  • Spots or containers
  • Items
  • Item location details
  • Other user-created data linked to your account

No longer have access to the app?

If you can no longer access Ploksa, you can request account deletion by email. Please email us from your registered Ploksa email address and include:

• Registered Ploksa email address

• Request type: Delete Ploksa account

• Optional reason for deletion

Send your request to support@ploksa.com.

Processing time

We aim to process account deletion requests within 7 to 30 days after verifying the request.

Data retention

Some limited information may be retained temporarily where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup, or compliance purposes. Any retained data will not be used for active app functionality.

For questions about account deletion or privacy, contact us at support@ploksa.com.
